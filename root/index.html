    What is cookies?

    Cookies are small texts comprised of the downloaded information which may be stored in your web browser or other devices that are connected to the internet (e.g., your computer, smartphone, or tablet) and retrievable by the server later. It is similar to the memory unit of the webpage.

    Cookies function helps us automatically collect and store information about your application usage and/or website visits as follows:

    • Internet domain and IP address from the point of your access to the application
  • Type of browser, software, as well as structure and operating system used for access to the application, date and time of your access to the application
  • Other website address linking you to our application, and
  • Webpage you access, visit and taking you from our application, including the content on the webpage you visit and the period of your visit.

    • Our Privacy Policy will determine all details about other data we collect and the how we use your data.

    Cookie usage

    Types of cookies we use and the method of using them include, but not limited to, the following

    • Strictly Necessary Cookies: This type of cookies is important for the functions of application, including the cookies that enable you to access the data and use our application safely.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: This type of cookies will enable us to remember and count the number of visitors to the application and help us know the behaviors of visiting the application to improve the application functions for better quality and appropriateness. Also, these cookies will compile statistics on access and behaviors of visiting the application which helps with the improvement of application. Users will be allowed to search what they want very easily. It enables us to understand user interests and measures effectiveness of our advertising.
  • Functionality Cookies: This type of cookies is used for recognizing your identity when you return to use the application. The purpose is to make sure that our application can respond to your needs effectively and remember your setup such as language, region or font size selected by you.
  • Targeting Cookies: This type of cookies records you visits to the website and application, webpages and links you have visited. We will use this data to adjust the website and content appearing on the web to match with your interests. Moreover, we share this information to the third party for that purpose, and
  • Advertising Cookies: This type of cookies will remember your setup when accessing the application and the data will be used to improve the application home page to offer you with the most appropriate advertising such as showing the advertising that you may be interested in, preventing or limiting the frequency of your exposure to the website of the same ads to measure effectiveness of the ads.

    • Please note that some types of cookies are managed by the third party such as advertising network, functions e.g., videos, maps, and social media, and other external website providers such as analysis of website visit, etc. These cookies are mostly analytical cookies/functionality cookies or targeting cookies. You should study the Cookies Policy and Privacy Policy on the third-party website to understand how your personal data will be used.
